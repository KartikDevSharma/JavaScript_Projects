Welcome, Space Cadet!

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to decipher alien codes and save the galaxy!

How to Play:

  1. Memorize the sequence of alien symbols shown.
  2. The symbols will be hidden after a brief moment.
  3. Recreate the sequence by clicking on the symbols below.
  4. Complete the sequence before time runs out!
  5. With each level, the code gets longer and time shorter.
